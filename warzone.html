about warzone:

Last updated: 8/15/2024

Warzone in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 is a large-scale, free-to-play battle royale mode that allows players to compete in massive multiplayer matches. Set within a sprawling map, players engage in combat, loot for weapons and resources, and fight to be the last team or individual standing. The mode emphasizes strategic gameplay, with features like the shrinking safe zone and the use of vehicles for traversal. Warzone in Modern Warfare 3 continues the core mechanics of previous installments, incorporating refined gunplay, enhanced graphics, and new in-game events that affect the dynamics of each match.
