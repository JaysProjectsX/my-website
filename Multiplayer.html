about multiplayer:

Last updated: 8/15/2024

The Multiplayer mode in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 offers a fast-paced, competitive online experience where players engage in various game modes like Team Deathmatch, Domination, and Search and Destroy. It features a wide array of weapons, perks, and killstreaks that players can unlock and customize to fit their playstyle. The gameplay focuses on quick reflexes, map control, and teamwork, with iconic maps that provide intense close-quarters and medium-range combat. Modern Warfare 3 multiplayer builds on the franchise's core mechanics while introducing new features like Strike Packages and weapon proficiency, allowing for deeper tactical options and personalization.
